RSVP to Google's Pre-Conference Workshop/Dinner!
Google will be hosting a Technical Interview Workshop from 5pm to 8pm on Friday, November 7 at the Georgia Tech Conference Center.

The workshop is aimed at students studying Computer Science or related majors. The session, led by Google engineers, will give you insight into what the industry looks for in a resume, tips on the Technical interview process and an opportunity to try your hand at sample questions on a whiteboard. Dinner will be available after the workshop for participants.

This workshop is by invite only. Please RSVP today to reserve your space. If you have any questions related to the content of the workshop or eligibility questions, please email Hal Marz - halm@google.com
