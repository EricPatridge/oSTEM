Facebook Group for the oSTEM Trans Working Group
Based on discussions from the 2013 National Conference, oSTEM now has a Facebook group dedicated to its trans members! It will allow you to better connect with one another, and with the TWG leadership.

In order to foster a safer environment, we ask that only trans people join. As we are just starting the group, it will be a Closed group, meaning that if you are a member of the group your name will be visible, but your posts will not. This will make it easier to find as we build the group. After this year’s National Conference we’ll have a discussion on whether a Closed group continues to meet your needs, or if we should change it to a Secret group where no one can see your name on the group.

We’re looking forward to getting to know you better!
