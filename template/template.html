Conference Info
oSTEM goes to OUTC
oSTEM attended the Out for Undergraduate Technology Conferene (OUTC) at Twitter HQ in San Francisco this past weekend. Attended by 176 bright LGBTQ Computer Science students from across the country, attendees learned about the latest technologies, tools and networked with the world's leading technology companies. Adam Stoffel and AP Fritts talked with students throughout the day about oSTEM and the benefits of joining their local chapter or the opportunity to start their own!
New Scientist Ad
oSTEM is pleased to announce, that we have a full page spread in the Oct. 18th issue of the New Scientist. This is a big step for our organization, and it's really amazing to see oSTEM featured in such a publication!
